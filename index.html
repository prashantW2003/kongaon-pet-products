Our Story

Born from a real problem in Kongaon.

I have a cat. And every time she needed food, I had to travel to another city to buy it. It was frustrating, expensive and time-consuming.

I looked around Kongaon — no pet store. So I decided to fix that. Kongaon Pet Products was born for every pet owner who felt the same frustration.

"I had to go to another city just to buy my cat's food. That's when I decided — Kongaon needs this."

— Founder, Kongaon Pet Products
